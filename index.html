About

A bit about me

I've been somewhere in the digital universe since 1997. Started as a 3D animator and visual artist, then stumbled into web development in the early 2000s when the internet was still figuring itself out — we had that in common. That eventually grew into a career spanning UI/UX design, web development, and running my own boutique studio since 2009.

What you'll find here isn't my day job — it's my hobby shelf. My GitHub is where I solve personal problems with code: a couple of Swift apps for macOS built out of genuine necessity, one of which apparently needed a Windows port too. I also dabble in home automation and DIY electronics, because apparently I don't believe in free time.

I'm a fixer by nature — find a problem, overthink it, then build something unnecessarily elegant to solve it. Outside of screens, I share my life with a senior Beagle named Bugz, and we have more in common than I'd like to admit: stubborn, persistent, and deeply unreasonable about food. I like to think she learned all three from me. She would disagree, loudly, then go back to sleep.
